Only Fresh Ingredients

GO HABANERO hotsauce is a hand crafted sauce made from only the freshest ingredients. Georgia peaches give the sauce a wonderful sweetness that balances out the heat from the blazing hot, vine ripened habanero peppers. Produced and packaged locally in Rochester Minnesota, this sauce is sure to become one of your favorites. Master flavor engineer Dean Steel is hard at work crafting new GO HABANERO flavor varieties that will make your tongue do a happy dance.

Made With Care

This bold flavor enhancer is a sauce that has humble roots. Established in 2013 when founder, Dean Steele, was experimenting in his kitchen with habanero peppers he had just picked from his family garden. The result was a delightfully unique sauce that brings life to nearly any dish. It was a true hit at the Steele family barbecues that summer and after many requests from friends, GO HABANERO went into full scale production.
