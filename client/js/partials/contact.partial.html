GO HABANERO hotsauce is a hand crafted sauce made from only the freshest ingredients. Georgia peaches give the sauce a wonderful sweetness that balances out the heat from the blazing hot, vine ripened habanero peppers.

Produced and packaged locally in Rochester Minnesota, this sauce is sure to become one of your favorites. Master flavor engineer Dean Steel is hard at work crafting new GO HABANERO flavor varieties that will make your tongue do a happy dance.
