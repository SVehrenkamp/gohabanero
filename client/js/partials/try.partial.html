Our Sauces

GO HABANERO hotsauce is a hand crafted sauce made from only the finest ingredients. A combination of fruits give these sauces a wonderful sweetness that balances out the heat from the blazing hot, vine ripened habanero peppers. Produced and packaged locally in Rochester Minnesota, this sauce is sure to become one of your favorites.

Master flavor engineer Dean Steel is hard at work crafting new GO HABANERO flavor varieties that will make your tongue do a happy dance.

Sweet Peach

Our flagship sauce, contains a blend of peaches that makes for the perfect balance of sweet and hot. Great on pizza and almost everything else!

Jalpeno Pear

Our signature green sauce contains pears, jalapenos and habaneros for extra heat. Great on chicken!

Hot 'n' Fresh

Don't be scared off by the color of our red sauce, Hot 'n' Fresh is a tomato based sauce that is great with breakfast!
